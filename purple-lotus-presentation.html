Key Insights

What the Data Tells Us

🪷

Purple Lotus

01

Round Down Is Your Backbone

$434K in gross profit from 16,632 orders. This single promo drives 49% of all gross profit. Protect it at all costs.

02

Blue Chip Flower = Highest ROI

Consistent 70–74% margins across all Blue Chip deals. Most profitable per dollar of revenue in the entire catalog.

03

10% Off 1st & 3rd Visit Is a Hidden Gem

1,276 orders at 56.8% margin with only a 10% discount. Best loyalty ROI — minimal cost, high return.

04

Jeeter & Stiiizy Drive Volume, Not Margin

3,500+ combined orders but 40–48% margins. Great for traffic and acquisition — monitor closely for profitability.

05

Deep Discounts Are Compressing Profit

Promos at 35–50% off (Turn, Chill Guy, Custom Dollar, 40% Jeeter) are generating volume but hurting the bottom line.

06

$721K in Discounts = Optimization Opportunity

27.8% of gross sales given away in discounts. Redirecting 10% toward bundle deals could recover $50K–$100K monthly.

Review 40–50% Off Promotions

Bundle deals (2 For $X) consistently outperform flat % discounts on margin. Shift strategy for key brands.
